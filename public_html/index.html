Crochet

/ˈkrəʊʃeɪ,ˈkrəʊʃi/

Derived from the French term which literally translates to “small hook”.
The name actually refers to the process of creating fabric by interlocking loops of materials with a crochet hook.

About the Designer

With the love for the handicraft and passion for revival of crochet as a craft, Juhi Choksi launched JustCrochet in November 2019, amalgamated the fineness of crocheting with innovative designs.
