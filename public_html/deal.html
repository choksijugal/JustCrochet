Why go Crochet?

Crochet is a process of creating textiles by using a crochet hook to interlock loops of yarn. The delicate art of crocheting is a lifelong skill that one uses to make beautiful items for home and wardrobe. Crochet is forever not for just now. Here is where Crochet excels – there’s no need for an upgrade as it is perfect already. There is no need to keep up with the trends as a handmade object is unique. Even if it’s one of a series, each one will be slightly different from the next. It’s the minute imperfections that make it so desirable. Each handmade piece of work is as unique as each person in the world.

Secondly, buying crochet products supports local people. The price you pay for it is exactly what you see – there are no hidden costs. Next, Buying crochet keeps its craft skills alive. We are facing lost skills, the demise of handcraft and a real loss of cultural identity. Buying crochet ensures traditional craft skills are kept alive and creates a demand for education in these skills.

Handmade crochet celebrates who we are. Crochet products from JustCrochet are a celebration of our contemporary lives and our living culture – people specific culture where one-fits-one. It’s about the time and effort that goes into each piece of work. It’s about the skill of maker, the technical ingenuity of the maker, the magic of individual’s imagination and it’s a treasure on a beach of throwaway machine-made dump!
